Dystopia

How to play

  • Move: WASD
  • Attack: J
  • Use active item: K
  • Interact: E. Interact with the statue to unlock powers. Interact with the stone circle to enter the dungeon
  • Pause: P
  • Accept: Space
  • FullScreen: F11
